# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us via email at **movilextra3@gmail.com**. We take security seriously and will respond promptly.

## Communication
For any questions or concerns, reach out to us at **movilextra3@gmail.com**.

**Disclaimer:** Any security-related communication should be directed to the email above. Do not share sensitive information publicly.  
Thank you for helping us keep our project secure!
